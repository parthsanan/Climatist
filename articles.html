The Fundamentals of Climate Change


Climate comprises of multiple aspects of our environment including humidity, atmospheric pressure, and temperature. Over the last century, humans have most definitely made their mark on planet Earth by, what seems like, doing everything they can to destroy it.

Because of this change in the climate, scientists around the world were in a frenzy, and as a result, terms like “Global Warming” and “The Greenhouse Effect” were born and garnered immense traction, rightfully so.

Today, a large proportion of people across the globe are aware of the Climate Crisis going on, but seem to rely mostly on organizations like the United Nations and NASA to be the ones dealing with this threat.
